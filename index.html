How It Works?

It starts from YOU!

  • Step 1: Sign In

    Create an account or log in to start the process of donating food.

  • Step 2: Fill Form for Leftover Food

    Provide details about the leftover food you wish to donate by filling out a simple form.

  • Step 3: Quality Check and Allocation

    We will assess the quality of the food and determine whether it is suitable for humans or animals.

  • Step 4: Volunteer Pickup

    Volunteers will come to your location to pick up the food donation at a scheduled time.
